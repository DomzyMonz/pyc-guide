Getting Started

Making a Bot

To get started, you should make a bot. This allows you to interact with the Discord API, and make your imagination a real thing!

Creating a bot is really straightforward.

  • Log in the Discord website.
  • Navigate to the Applications page or click this link.
  • Find and click the "New Application" button.
  • Give your application a name and click "Create".
  • Go to the "Bot" tab and click "Add Bot". Then, click "Yes, do it!" to create the bot.

You should have made a Discord bot!
